Harrogate Atttractions

The everyman building is more known for being a cinema than a shopping complex which it is also. The shopping complex includes restaurants suchas Yo Sushi, CAU and the Veeno wine bar. The cinema includes things like sofas and a service in which you can ask for pizza to be delivered to your seat mid movie.

The Hydro is a swimming centre, which also comes with a gym, restaurant and an option to hire the place out for a birthday party in which the Hydro provides inflatables to use.

The climbing centre is perfect for anyone who wants to know how to learn to climb, as is one if not the most popular place in the area to learn to climb or get better at climbing.

The valley gardens is a massive area in which a park, multiple tennis courts, golf and crazy golf course, swimming pool, cafe and pinewoods. This is a very popular location in Harrogate and is only a 10 minute walk from the town centre so is very convenient.
